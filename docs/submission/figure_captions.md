# Figure Captions

**Figure 1.** Problem structure. (a) Spatial superstructure: eight Metro Manila local-government-unit catchments (circles), four candidate plant sites with discrete capacity levels (squares), and four product streams; stage-one decisions select sites, sizes, gas-energy-recovery modules, and catchment contracts, while stage-two flows respond per scenario under the RA 11898 footprint-based recovery obligation. (b) Verified site flowsheet (configuration v3): yield-shift reactor on the Genuino superposition and Westerhout kinetics, quench and flash, 45 °C stabilizer, atmospheric naphtha column, and vacuum diesel/wax column.

**Figure 2.** Liquid recovery over 200 compositions drawn from the uncertainty set at two model fidelities, against the 80% obligation (dashed). Reactor-level superposition gives P(recovery ≥ 80%) = 38%; the full validated flowsheet, where flash and stabilizer losses and unconverted material count against recovery, gives 24%.

**Figure 3.** Parity of the reduced-order oracle against six rigorous DWSIM simulations spanning the uncertainty set (24 product-flow cells), before (left) and after (right) the two-parameter recalibration. Shaded band: ±5%. Post-fit worst error 1.6%; leave-one-case-out worst error 1.87%.

**Figure 4.** Gaussian-process surrogate calibration on held-out data. Left: predicted versus oracle recovery with 95% predictive intervals. Right: standardized residuals against the standard normal; empirical 95%-interval coverage 0.99.

**Figure 5.** Cost of assuring P(recovery ≥ 80%) in at least 90 of 100 scenarios, relative to the risk-neutral optimum, across ten sample-average-approximation replications per spatial-heterogeneity regime (log scale). Point color identifies the assurance mechanism: hardware hedging, selective contracting, or exit. Mild regime: 18.3 ± 9.9 MPHP per year, hardware only. Strong regime: median 413 MPHP per year with the mechanism switching.

**Figure 6.** Global sensitivity of net present value (Sobol first-order and total indices). Feedstock oil yield, equivalently composition, carries a total index of 0.62, exceeding all product prices and the ±30% capital range combined.
